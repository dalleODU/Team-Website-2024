Problem Characteristics

Financial Literacy

A lack of financial literacy contributes to growing debt and confusion for young adults, including:

  • Difficulty creating a realistic budget.
  • Uncertainty about where their money is going.
  • Struggles with prioritizing expenses.
  • Lack of understanding about how late payments affect their financial future.

A Lack of Options

A Lack of well-rounded financial options results in issues such as:

  • Relying on multiple seperate financial apps to manage finances.
  • Increased difficulty staying organized, making financial management harder than it should be.
  • A blurred vision of long-term financial goals.

Forgotten Payments

Forgotten monthly payments lead to a range of life-altering issues, including:

  • Rising interest rates.
  • Late fees and penalties.
  • A declining credit score.
    • Difficulty making significant purchases.
  • Additional time required to pay off debt.
  • Negative impacts on physical and mental health.
