The Problem

Credit plays a significant role in personal finances, yet many people have struggled to pay their debts on time, budget their income, and save money.

In Fact

  • U.S. consumers altogether owe over $1 trillion in credit card debt. (Horymski)
    • This is roughly equivalent to Sweden's national debt amount. (CEIC data)
  • And the average balance per person rose to $6,501. (Horymski)

Why is This Happening?

  • Forgotten monthly payments.
  • Lack of proper budgeting skills.
  • No integration of budgeting and debt management features in mobile applications.

The Solution: CreditTrax

A smartphone app that simplifies financial management by combining debt tracking, AI-driven budgeting suggestions, and visual tools to help users see their progress.

With timely payment reminders and personalized advice, CreditTrax helps users stay on top of their bills while forming smarter financial habits.

How CreditTrax Will Help

  • designed to give users a clear, organized view of their financial obligations
  • users can input all their debts e.g. credit card balances and student loans along with their respective interest rates and due dates
  • prompts the user to manually approve payment of a debt, and if they neglect to do so, the app will issue increasing reminders
